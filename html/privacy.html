The Magic Notebook Privacy Policy

If you are seeing this message, the Magic Notebook has been set up, but the privacy policy has not been set up. Please contact the administrator and request a privacy policy to be set up.

Entering something into the Magic Notebook is like putting something in a box, marking it as "private", and putting a lightweight lock onto it. Honorable people will leave it alone, and casual snoopers won't get in, but anyone who comes with a crowbar will be able to open it. The Magic Notebook is designed to afford basic protection, but not to repel serious attack; you should therefore not use it to store credit card information or other sensitive material. Don't put anything in that it would be bad for other people to be able to break in.

What should you use it for? Well, here are a few things:

  • Projects you're working on
  • Notes on different topics.
  • Jokes
  • Quotes
  • Recipes
  • Websites to visit
  • Friends to write/e-mail/call
  • A to-do list of things you'd like to work on.
  • Places to visit
  • Books to read
  • Etc.

Enjoy!
